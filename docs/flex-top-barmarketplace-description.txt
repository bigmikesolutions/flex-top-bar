ONE-LINE PITCH (plugin directory summary)
-----------------------------------------
Stop losing attention above the fold. Flex Top Bar gives you a modern visual editor, live preview, and pro layouts designed to help your promos, links, and CTAs get noticed.


LONG DESCRIPTION (main listing body)
------------------------------------

Your site only gets one first impression — and most visitors decide in seconds. 
Flex Top Bar turns that tiny strip of screen into a high-visibility space for announcements, sales, trust signals, social proof, and contact paths,
all in a polished bar that’s designed to match your brand.

Forget clunky forms and “save” marathons. The admin is a fast, modern experience with live preview so you can iterate quickly while you work.
Changes save automatically, helping you stay in flow and publish updates faster.

BUILD BARS THAT WORK AS HARD AS YOU DO

• Multiple bars — Run separate campaigns, audiences, or messages without hacking your theme.
• Mix-and-match columns — Combine rich text, social icons, and contact blocks in one responsive row. Resize columns, align content left/center/right, and reorder by drag-and-drop.
• Text that moves people — Static message, or eye-catching motion: slider, fade-in, and blink effects for multi-line messaging when you need the extra punch.
• Social & contact, done right — Major social platforms with flexible icon styles and colors; contact rows for email, phone, location, chat, and more — styled to match your bar.
• Top or bottom placement, your rules — Pin to the top or bottom, control borders and colors, and optionally hide the bar on scroll for a cleaner reading experience.
• Mobile isn’t an afterthought — Control whether message content shows on small screens, column by column.
• Draft vs. published — Tune in private, then publish when it’s perfect. Know at a glance when live visitors haven’t seen your latest changes yet.
• Schedule visibility (where included in your plan) — Show the bar only in the window that matters for launches, holidays, and limited-time offers.

WHO IT’S FOR

Store owners, agencies, membership sites, and anyone who is tired of “good enough” announcement plugins. If your message matters, your bar should look and behave like a product — not an afterthought.

Install Flex Top Bar. Claim the strip of screen your competitors ignore — and make it easier for visitors to notice what matters most.
